First Year at 37signals

Highs

  • Easymon
  • Easy Button (Intermission + mysql_ip_role_swap)
  • Read Write Splitting push

Lows

Spring. For some reason, I found it incredibly difficult to get traction in the spring of this year. I feel like my output was way down, and I wasn't pulling my own weight. :(

Goals

  • Keep a daily journal.

    Even if it's just one entry per day, write down what I did so I keep mentally evaluating what and how much I'm doing. If I feel I'm not contributing enough, review my journal to see if that's unfounded. If it's not, crank it up a notch or two.

  • Hit Nagios Zero when I'm on call.

    This is important so we don't get a lot of noise that we keep ignoring. Personal experience validates this observation. If we see it every day, we forget about it.

  • Push my contributions during budget cycles to 20% rather than 14%.

    Since I still internalize all of my failures and everyone else's successes , I want to push myself to be a very productive member of the team.

  • Turn it off.

    I tend to stay on all the time, which causes my kids to say things like: "Dad working at Basecamp is just the same as working at UPS." I need to plan some family time as family time, and take off for it.
